This is our 7th year doing the college football pool. For those of you who have played in seasons past, thank you and I hope you continue to play each year! For those of you who are college football (or simply football) fans, I encourage you to join our pick’em pool. This is a fun and affordable way to essentially win money potentially every week based on your picks.

    Rules:
  • $55 entrance fee for the entire year, must be paid before deadline or late fee
  • Each week you will pick 10 game spreads
  • Game of the week (1 pick) – Every week will feature a popular game that everybody must pick from. You can suggest games but typically they’re top ranked teams, old rivalries or simply games that people are interested in.
  • Locked (3 picks) – Pick 3 spreads that you are most confident will cover the spread, Locked picks are worth double
  • Regular Picks (6 picks) – Pick 6 spreads for regular points
  • The weekly spreads are updated Mon/Tues and must be submitted before Friday 11:59PM. Source of spread's TBD
  • Return your picks by submitting your personal Excel file
    Points & Tie Breakers:
  • There are weekly winners as well as season overall points winner.
  • Locks = 4pt
  • Regular games = 2pt
  • Tied Spread = ½ , locks = 2 and regular = 1
  • You can lock Game of the Week (G.O.T.W)
  • Season points get added each week, however your lowest (1) score will be dropped
  • In the event of a tie for weekly points, tie breaker will be ‘Game of the Week’. The weekly cash will be split among those who picked the winner of G.O.T.W spread. If none of the tied players chose the correct G.O.T.W winner then there is no tie breaker.
    Payouts:
  • Payouts go to the weekly winner.
  • Payouts are handed out to first and second season points winners. Payout determined by the number of particpants.

After the spreads are sent out each week it is your responsibility to remember to get your picks in by Friday midnight. There won’t be any reminders. With your lowest score dropped, you can afford to miss a week.

Last year we started using www.leaguesafe.com for our season payments. There was a 3% fee, $1.65, to use this application but I felt that we got all of our payments in faster and cash was paid soon after the last bowl game. Typically payments have been delayed since there are hold-outs. We’ll continue to use this method for payment. If you win then the money gets paid back to you in a varied of ways; PayPal, direct deposit, check-in mail. I can’t recall from last year but I believe some methods of collecting had small fees. This has not been set up yet, more info to come soon. Payments are due the Friday before the first week. There is a $5 late fee that Leaguesafe has built-in if you’re late. For those who do pay late, your season points will count but if you win a week without paying you forfeit your earnings that week to 2nd place.

As with last year there is a small commissioners fee. This pool takes a lot of my personal time so this covers my beer and pizza. Last year it was $25 total and it will probably stay the same. I’ve used this commissioner fee to also help cover certain costs in the past.

I hope you guys decide to join for another exciting year! For those who haven’t played before it’s similar to NFL fantasy leagues where your interest is greater in more games. No longer will you be cheering for your favorite team but the other 10 games you’ve got money on. It changes how you watch college football, and if you’re one of our Canadians that means actually watching college football now.
